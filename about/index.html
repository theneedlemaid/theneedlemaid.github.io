About

Story

With a bright future ahead in consulting, I had an insight and mountains got me calling. They were home. Literally. I hail from a small town, right at the foothills of the Himalayas in the North-Eastern India. Known for the best tea in the world, Darjeeling is lush with green flora & beautiful hillscapes with a sight to behold.

Our fabric is sourced from the nearby areas including Tibet, Bhutan and some land of the mystics.

Invictus by William Ernest Henley

      Black as the pit from pole to pole,
I thank whatever gods may be

      I am the captain of my soul.
